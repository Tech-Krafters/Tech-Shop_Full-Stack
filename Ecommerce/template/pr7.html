Specifications


Featuring a striking design that has been inspired by the collaboration with BMW Designworks Group, this ASUS laptop is the ideal computing device for all gamers. The raised ridges above the rear exhaust enhance the aesthetic appeal of this gaming laptop. It comes with an offset cut below the display, an asymmetrical edge, and more which further add to its aesthetic appeal.

This gaming laptop comes with an optional expanded light bar which makes gaming fun and interesting. The rim of this laptop’s chassis features diffused RGB LEDs that wrap around it, illuminating the laptop’s front and side edges. The colors and effects of this light bar can be easily customized, according to your preference. Not only that, but you can also customize the illumination across all the compatible Aura Sync peripherals, thereby creating the perfect gaming ambiance wherever you are.

Thanks to the narrow bezels, this gaming laptop offers a wider screen area, making it a complete delight to play games, watch videos, and more. The combination of the narrow bezels and screen-to-body ratio of up to 81.5% helps you enjoy an immersive viewing experience, whether you’re playing games or watching movies on a weekend.

This laptop comes with smooth scissor-door hinges that appear when its lid has been lifted. These hinges disappear into the chassis once the lid has been closed. This design offers additional space for cooling and venting around the heatsinks, thereby helping the laptop stay cool even when you’re playing graphic-intensive games. As the hinges are not exposed when the lid is shut, the chances of them getting nicks or cracks are greatly reduced.

Thanks to the refresh rate of 144 Hz, this laptop helps you play fast-paced games seamlessly. The high-FPS ensures that you won't experience screen stuttering or delay, making it a delight to play first-person shooter games, racing games, and more.
